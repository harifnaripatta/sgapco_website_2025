About

Who We Are

Leading the Way in Aluminum Solutions Since 1981

SAUDI GERMAN ALUMINUM PRODUCTS CO., LTD (SGAPCO) was first conceived in 1977 and officially formed in 1981 with foreign participation, focusing on the transfer of know-how. SGAPCO was created to meet the high standards of aluminum products demanded by both national and international contractors, providing optimum solutions for their needs.

Since the completion of its plant in 1981, SGAPCO has grown into one of the leading manufacturers of premium aluminum products including curtain walls, partitions, thermo-insulated doors and windows, facades, structural glazing, automatic doors, rolling shutters, grilles, and venetian blinds.

  • Custom Architectural Aluminium Systems
  • Innovative Product Design and Manufacturing
  • Adaptable and Forward-Thinking Management
